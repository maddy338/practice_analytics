Experiences & Amenities

From guided hikes to spa treatments, discover all the ways to make your stay unforgettable.

On-Site Amenities

Infinity Pool

Our heated infinity pool overlooks the valley and is open year-round. Poolside service available from 11am to 6pm daily.

Wellness Spa

Full-service spa offering massages, facials, body treatments, and couples packages. Reservations recommended.

Fitness Center

24-hour access to cardio equipment, free weights, and yoga mats. Daily yoga classes offered at 7am and 5pm.

The Overlook Restaurant

Farm-to-table dining featuring seasonal Appalachian cuisine. Open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Reservations required for dinner.

Summit Bar

Craft cocktails, local wines, and mountain views. Live acoustic music on Friday and Saturday evenings.

Fire Pit Terrace

Gather around our outdoor fire pits each evening. S'mores kits available from the front desk.

Guided Experiences

Sunrise Hike

Join our naturalist guide for a moderate 4-mile hike to catch the sunrise from Hawk's Peak. Includes hot coffee and pastries at the summit.

$45 per person

Waterfall Tour

Half-day adventure to three hidden waterfalls in the Pisgah National Forest. Transportation and picnic lunch provided.

$85 per person

Farm Visit

Tour the local organic farms that supply our restaurant. Meet the farmers, sample fresh produce, and enjoy a farm-fresh lunch.

$65 per person

Stargazing Night

Our resident astronomer guides you through the night sky using our professional telescope. Hot chocolate and blankets provided.

$35 per person
